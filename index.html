Farmer requests pick up from her/his farm on the app

Driver recieves Request

Transport

Sell

Farmer receives payment

When the vehicle goes for the pickup the very next day, farmer receives payment for the last day's vegetable

Kickstarting the rural economy

Farmlift helps farmers to sell their fresh vegetables in the local market on the daily basis through its platform. Farmers do not have to wait for other farmers to harvest their vegetables in order to arrange transport in a cooperative manner to reduce costs and increase safety. Farmlift helps farmers in earning a small amount everyday which adds up in a significant sum of money at the end of every month. Creating a daily source of income is crucial to farmers who rarely see any continuous income throughout the year, as it provides them with the liquidity which they can spend on daily needs

About Us

Farmlift provides a platform to small and marginal farmers to transport their vegetables to the market. Transporting vegetables to the market is a big challenge for small and marginal farmers who do not produce sufficient amount to hire or own a vehicle for transportation. Usually, wholesale markets for vegetables where these farmers sell their produce operate during the night hours which makes the accessibility of market even more difficult. A farmer who works for the entire day is not left with enough energy to take the vegetable to the market during night and sell it there.

Farmlift has created a solution where our truck goes door to door to pick up vegetables from farmers irrespective of the quantity they plucked that day. Our truck then goes to the market during the night hours while the farmer can have a sound sleep at her/his home with her/his family. A farmer need not to bother about selling her/his produce, Farmlift takes care of the entire process from pick up of vegetable till the farmer gets the money for her/his produce.
Farmlift has been a game changer for a lot farmers as they can now focus on producing vegetables instead of bothering about its transportation and sale. Also, Farmlift has completely removed the risk of life threatening accidents that a farmer might face during the night hours due to stress, lack of sleep etc. while transporting vegetables.
Farmlift is also closely working with farmers to improve existing farming practices to make agriculture more sustainable and profitable for farmers. We are committed to create a positive impact on the life of small and marginal farmers of India.
